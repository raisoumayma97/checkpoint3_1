Chocolate Cake

Chocolate cake is a cake flavored with melted chocolate, cocoa powder, or both.It can also have other ingredients such as fudge.

Red Velvet Cake

Red velvet cake is traditionally a red, crimson, or scarlet-colored layer cake, layered with ermine icing or cream cheese icing.

Carrot Cake

Carrot cake is a sweet and moist spice cake, full of cut carrots and toasted nuts, and covered in cream cheese icing.

Lemon Cake

Lemon cake is a dense, flavorful and very popular cake. Lemon is used to provide its unique flavor to a variety of cakes.
